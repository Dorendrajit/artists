Alan Menken

Songwriter,Composer

 UNITED STATES


Alan Menken is an acclaimed American composer, songwriter, and conductor, best known for his work on Disney animated films. He has played a pivotal role in the Disney Renaissance, creating memorable scores and songs for classics like The Little Mermaid, Beauty and the Beast, and Aladdin. His contributions to film and musical theater have earned him numerous accolades, including multiple Academy Awards, Grammy Awards, and Tony nominations.

Throughout his career, Menken has demonstrated a remarkable ability to craft emotionally resonant and catchy melodies that have become integral parts of popular culture. His collaborations with lyricists such as Howard Ashman and Tim Rice have produced some of the most beloved songs in the Disney canon. Beyond his work with Disney, Menken has also composed for Broadway shows and other film projects, showcasing his versatility as a musical storyteller.
